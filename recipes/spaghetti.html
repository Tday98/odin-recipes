Homemade Spaghetti

Description

In this classic spaghetti recipe, flavorful noodles are used with a rich pasta sauce and meatballs

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Meatballs

Directions

  1. Boil pasta: In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles per the recipe below.
  2. Prepare sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce (I love this brand) & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
